Our Journey

This project started when I bought a vintage trailer for $500 and remodeled it entirely using free reclaimed materials. Owning the land where the trailer sits allows me to earn consistent rental income without monthly lot rent expenses.

The goal of this app is to help others understand how a small investment in trailers can be turned into either a one-time profit by selling or long-term recurring income through rentals.
