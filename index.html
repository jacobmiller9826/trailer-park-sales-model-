Sales & Rental Model Explanation

Own the land? Huge advantage! You can either sell a trailer for a one-time profit or rent it for consistent income.

  • Purchase Cost: Cost to acquire trailer (e.g., $500)
  • Remodel Cost: Cost to remodel trailer ($0 if using reclaimed materials)
  • Maintenance: Monthly upkeep (e.g., $50–$100)
  • Selling Price: Expected sale price (e.g., $5,000–$10,000)
  • Monthly Rent: Amount charged if rented (e.g., $700/month)

Profit Calculation:
Selling profit = Selling Price - (Purchase Cost + Remodel Cost)
Renting profit = Monthly Rent - Maintenance. Break-even shows how quickly you recover your investment.
